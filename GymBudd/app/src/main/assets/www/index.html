Limitations of the BMI

Your BMI can tell you if you're carrying too much weight but it can't tell if you're carrying too much fat. The BMI can't tell the difference between excess fat, muscle, or bone. The adult BMI does not take into account age, gender or muscle mass. This means that: very muscular adults and athletes may be classed "overweight" or "obese" even though their body fat is low adults who lose muscle as they get older may fall in the "healthy weight" range even though they may be carrying excess fat
